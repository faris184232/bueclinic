GET TO KNOW BUE DENTAL CLINICS

WHERE ARTISTERY MEETS EXPERTISE

BUE Clinic was established in the year 2005.
Dr. Mai El Banna joined in and established
the specialized Dental department in the year 2007.
Dr. Hashem provided the lastest in dental technology
and a staff of the highest qualifications to produce the
utopia of dental surgeries.
BUE Dental Clinic offers its patients superior dental
care provided by specialized professionals, using up
to date equipment all
wrapped in satin ribbon of comfort and relaxation.
BUE Dental Clinic is a polyclinic, in a sense that
it offers all the dental specialties under one roof,
performed to conform

to international high standards of quality medical care.
As the patients compose our primary concern, BUE
Dental Clinic ensures that its patients are made fully
conscious of their ongoing condition through a detailed
explanation by a professional with the help of visual aids
and an expatiated treatment plan.We also plight the utmost
performance and results in the minimum amount
of visits possible; in other words, you get the creme
du la creme of dental service and save time doing it!
All of this, amalgamated with guaranteed results
and insurance certification makes BUE Dental Clinic
the ultimate place to achieve sublime dental care.
